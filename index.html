Questions contractors ask us

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. You get 14 days of full access with no payment info required. Enter a card only when you're ready to keep going past the trial.

What trades does Larch work for?

Plumbing, electrical, HVAC, roofing, landscaping, general contracting, handyman, painting, concrete/masonry, flooring, cleaning — anything where you run jobs, send quotes, and invoice customers.

Does Larch replace my accounting software?

Not entirely. Larch handles the operational side — jobs, quotes, invoices, customer SMS. Your accountant can still use QuickBooks or similar for tax filing. We'll add accounting integrations as we grow.

How does the AI SMS work?

When a customer texts your business number, Claude (the AI behind Larch) reads the message, checks your rules, and sends back an appropriate reply — book an appointment, answer a pricing question, or hand off to you. You always see the conversation and can jump in anytime. You can also disable AI entirely if you prefer.

Can I have my team use it too?

Yes. Invite staff with one of three roles: owner (full access), admin (can manage jobs/customers but not billing/team), or staff (restricted — you can hide financials, lock job deletion, etc.). Extra users are included in the $149/mo plan.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You can export all your data to Excel anytime. If you cancel, your data is retained for 30 days in case you want to come back, then permanently deleted.

Is my customer data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We never sell or share customer information. Read our privacy policy for the full details.

Who built Larch?

Larch is built and run by a small independent team. We talk to contractors weekly and ship improvements fast. Reach us at xpanselogic@gmail.com with feature requests.
